An Interactive Guide to Effect AI Tooling

Building an agent in @effect/ai looks almost suspiciously simple. Three primitives — Tool, Toolkit, LanguageModel — snap together and your model is suddenly calling functions in your code. The catch is that it took me embarrassingly long to figure out which piece was doing what. This page is the explanation I wish I'd had: poke each demo until it clicks.
